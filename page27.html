A thief and a beggar sat by the road. The difference between us, the thief said, is one of agency. While you passively wait for your lot to improve, I take steps to make it happen. Along the road came a wealthy merchant, his camel laden with spice. The thief stood up brandishing a knife and attacked. The merchant drew his dagger and fought back. When both men were dead, the beggar picked up the merchant’s cloak, and thief’s knife, and lived the rest of his days as a wealthy man.
